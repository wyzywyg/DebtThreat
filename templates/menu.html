Mechanics

Game Start: The player enters a unique username to start a new game or continue a previous game. The player starts with a budget of ₱500,000.

Selection

The player chooses a school and a course. Each course has a different tuition fee deducted from the initial budget. Each course also has a corresponding possible salary range, determined by the player's education points.

Yearly Scenarios

The game is divided into four parts, representing the four years of college. Each year presents different scenarios that can increase or decrease the player's education, health, and happiness points.

Health Bar

If the health bar drops below 10, the player must pay ₱10,000 for medical expenses.

Salary Calculation

The player’s monthly salary is determined by their education points:

1-20 points: Lowest monthly salary

21-40 points: Second lowest monthly salary

41-60 points: Medium monthly salary

61-80 points: High monthly salary

81-100 points: Highest monthly salary

Winning Condition

The player wins if the total of their first six months’ salary and the remaining money from the initial ₱500,000 is enough to repay the ₱500,000 debt.

Leaderboard

Players’ scores are ranked on a leaderboard, promoting competition.
